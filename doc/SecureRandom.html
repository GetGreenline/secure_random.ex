SecureRandom

  • Summary
  • Functions

Takes my favorite hits from Ruby’s SecureRandom and brings em to elixir. Mostly a convienance wrapper around Erlangs Crypo library, converting Crypto.strong_rand_bytes/1 into a string.

Examples

iex> SecureRandom.base64
"xhTcitKZI8YiLGzUNLD+HQ=="

iex> SecureRandom.urlsafe_base64(4)
"pLSVJw"

iex> SecureRandom.uuid
"a18e8302-c417-076d-196a-71dfbd5b1e03"
